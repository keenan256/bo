Who We Are

Welcome to the Business Owners Power Team, a dynamic subgroup within the BNI Sunrise chapter of Business Network International (BNI). We are a collective of passionate and driven entrepreneurs who each own and operate our unique businesses. Our team is dedicated to fostering a collaborative environment where members can share knowledge, support one another, and create powerful business synergies.

Our members come from diverse industries, including IT, legal, advertising, marketing, and more, each bringing their expertise and innovative approaches to the table. By working together, we aim to enhance our professional growth, expand our networks, and achieve new levels of success.

At the core of our team is the commitment to mutual support and growth. Through regular meetings, networking events, and collaborative projects, we leverage each other's strengths to overcome challenges and seize opportunities. Join us in our journey as we build a thriving community of business leaders who are making a significant impact in their respective fields.
